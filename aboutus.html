HISTORY

LVinnamon, a renowned cinnamon roll company, was established in the late 1970s by two childhood friends, Lucy and Victor. Inspired by their shared love for cinnamon and pastries, they embarked on a culinary adventure to create the perfect cinnamon roll. After years of experimentation and recipe refinement, they finally perfected their signature dough infused with a fragrant blend of cinnamon and spices.

With their mouthwatering creation in hand, they set up a small kiosk at a local farmer's market, where LVinnamon's irresistible aroma captured the hearts of passersby.

LVinnamon's journey from humble beginnings to a cinnamon roll empire is a testament to the founders' unwavering dedication and passion. As the company grew, so did their commitment to quality ingredients and traditional baking techniques.

COMPANY PROFILE

LVinnamon is a premier cinnamon roll company renowned for its irresistible, freshly baked treats that transport taste buds to a realm of pure indulgence. Established in 1995, LVinnamon has become synonymous with exceptional quality and unmatched flavor.

Our secret lies in the meticulous selection of premium ingredients, expertly blended with a perfect balance of cinnamon and spices. Each LVinnamon roll is handcrafted with love, ensuring a soft, fluffy texture and a delectable swirl of heavenly sweetness.

At LVinnamon, we take pride in our commitment to customer satisfaction. From our friendly and knowledgeable staff to our cozy and inviting stores, we strive to create an unforgettable experience for cinnamon lovers of all ages.
