Description
Cinnamon rolls are a delectable pastry treat known for their soft, fluffy texture and enticing aroma. These sweet, indulgent treats are made by rolling out a dough infused with cinnamon, sugar, and butter, resulting in a swirl of rich, cinnamon-infused layers. The dough used for cinnamon rolls is typically enriched with ingredients like butter, eggs, and milk, which contribute to its tender and pillowy texture. After rolling out the dough into a rectangular shape, a generous amount of cinnamon sugar mixture is spread evenly across the surface, followed by a layer of melted butter. This creates a luscious filling that infuses every bite with a sweet and aromatic cinnamon flavor.
